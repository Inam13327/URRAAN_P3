RED CARD 2018

China Digital Football Awards

The Red Card is globaly recognised as the leading annuaal report to assesses the most

influetial European football clubs, leagues and players online in Chine. Now in its 7th

year, Red Card 2018 has been reimagined to include 5 additional awards to recognise

the many area of digital performance in China. thereby taking the Red Card report to

a whole new level. Due to the nature and variety of these new categories. the winner

will be chosen by a panel of 6 industry expert,rather than by the traditional metrics

used to assess the three headline awards.



This report aims to educate team and organisations on how to improve their china

digital strtegy, what today's Chinese fan wants and what works online in China.
